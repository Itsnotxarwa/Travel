Bejaia,

Municipality in Algeria.

Bejaia, is an Algerian town located on the edge of the Mediterranean Sea, 220 km east of Algiers. It is the capital of the wilaya of Béjaïa and the daïra of Béjaïa, in Kabylia. Known in Roman times under the name of Saldae, it was promoted to capital of the vandal kingdom before being Islamized in the 8th century.
